Welcome to the Encryption-Decryption Tool!

Use this tool to securely encrypt and decrypt your text using three powerful algorithms:

  • Caesar Cipher - A classic shift cipher for encoding text.
  • Columnar Transposition - A more complex algorithm for secure encoding.
